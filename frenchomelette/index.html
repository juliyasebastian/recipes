French Omelette

Serves 1

2 eggs

2 tablespoons water

Freshly ground black pepper

1 tablespoon butter

Salt

1. place eggs, 2 tablespoons of water, black pepper and salt in a bowl and whisk lightly to combine.

2. Heat an omelette pan over a medium heat until hot. Add butter to the pan. Spread butter so the base is completely coated. Heat until the butter is foaming, but not browned. Add the egg mixture.

3. Use a palette knife or fork to gently draw up the edge of the omelette until no liquid remains and the omelette is lightly set.

4. Serve omelette plain, or topped with filling of your choice.

5. Fold in half.

6. Slip omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
